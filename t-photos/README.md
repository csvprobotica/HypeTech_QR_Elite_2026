# 👨‍💻 Meet Team HypeTech

## Introduction

The team has put their greatest effort into building the best possible robot. Our preparation for this olympiad has been built on extensive learning in construction, design, and programming. Long hours of track analysis, testing sessions, and strategic study have led to the creation of our own approach — **PROJECT H** — based on the components the team chose to work with, aiming for the best possible performance in every stage of the competition.

Throughout this document and the entire repository, all of the design, programming, and construction work behind our robot is explained in detail.
