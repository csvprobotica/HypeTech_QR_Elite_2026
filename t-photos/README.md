Team HypeTech QR Elite
====

This folder contains **Team HypeTech QR Elite's official photo**, which highlights our team spirit and our creative minds .

Team HypeTech QR Elite is made up of Jesus, Guillermo, and Angel, representing Colegio San Vicente de Paúl from Santiago de Veraguas, Panamá in the Future Engineers  Senior's category of the World Robot Olympiad. United by creativity, collaboration, and a passion for robotics, we are proud to showcase our work on this global stage.
